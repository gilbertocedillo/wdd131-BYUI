Personal Portfolio Site Plan

Final Project | WDD131

Purpose

This portfolio website will present my technical expertise in IT, cybersecurity, and interdisciplinary science. It will serve as a professional showcase for recruiters, hiring managers, and academic peers.

Audience

The primary audience includes recruiters and professors evaluating my skills. Secondary audiences include peers and community members seeking resources or inspiration.

Planned Dynamic Elements

  • Interactive project gallery with category filters.
  • Light/Dark theme toggle for accessibility.
  • Validated contact form with conditional error messages.
  • Motivational quote generator using arrays and randomization.

Logo

Minimalist GC monogram with shield/lock icon to symbolize cybersecurity and trust.
