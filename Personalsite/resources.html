Beginner Cybersecurity FAQ

What is phishing?

A fake email or message designed to trick users into giving away personal or financial information.

How long should a strong password be?

At least 12 characters and unique for every account.

What does HTTPS mean?

Your connection to the website is encrypted, providing better security than HTTP.

What is the safest way to use public Wi-Fi?

Use a VPN and avoid logging into sensitive accounts.
